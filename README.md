# AI Mock Interview Project

AI Mock Interviewer is an intelligent interview practice platform that simulates real interview scenarios to help candidates prepare more effectively. The application adapts to the job role, job description, and the user’s resume to deliver personalized and realistic interview sessions.

## About the Project

Interview preparation can be overwhelming when candidates are unsure what to expect. AI Mock Interviewer solves this by generating customized mock interviews using:

* Target job role
* Job description
* User resume

Based on these inputs, the system conducts structured interviews covering both technical and behavioral aspects. The platform helps users identify weaknesses, improve answer quality, and gain confidence through repeated practice.

## Core Features

* **Role-Specific Interviews**  
  Questions are generated according to the selected role and job description, aligning closely with real hiring expectations.
* **Resume-Based Questioning**  
  The system analyzes the uploaded resume to extract skills, projects, and experience, then challenges users on their own background.
* **Text and Voice Responses**  
  Users can answer questions by typing or using speech-to-text for a more realistic interview experience.
* **Instant AI Feedback**  
  Each response is evaluated for clarity, relevance, structure, and tone, with immediate feedback provided.
* **Performance Evaluation**  
  At the end of an interview session, users receive an overall score and actionable improvement suggestions.
* **Session History**  
  Past interview sessions can be saved and reviewed to track progress over time.

## Use Cases

* Students preparing for placements
* Fresh graduates entering the job market
* Professionals switching roles or domains
* Anyone looking to improve interview confidence and communication skills

## Objective

The goal of AI Mock Interviewer is to make interview preparation personalized, structured, and measurable, enabling users to approach real interviews with greater confidence and readiness.
